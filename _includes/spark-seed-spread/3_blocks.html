Investee Spotlight

Male Contraceptive Initiative

Dr. John DeCaro, a urologist from Augusta, identified a gap in knowledge and discussion around male contraception. Now a Spark-Seed-Seed investee, John is expanding awareness to male Veterans through a three-pronged approach known as the “Male Contraceptive Initiative.”

Innovation Spotlight

The Prosthetic Sock Management Tool (PSMT)

The PSMT can enhance a Veteran's prosthetic experience. Now available on the VA Intraprenuerial Product Marketplace, this two-part organizational system aids in understanding and health management for prosthetic sock use.

Alumni Spotlight

Minh Ho

Minh Ho, Acting Chief of Infectious Disease, Clinical Hospital Epidemiologist, and Staff Physician at Orlando VA Medical Center, and his team of pharmacists, social workers, and nurses are introducing new ways of thinking across VHA. Through harm reduction measures, Minh is striving to provide Veterans with safe and reliable access to life-saving tools, such as clean syringes, fentanyl test strips, and wound care products.
