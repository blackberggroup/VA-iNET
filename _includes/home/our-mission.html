Our Mission

VHA Innovators Network (iNET) is a bustling community of employees united by a desire to solve healthcare problems and engage in shared learning. Our specialists facilitate local programming, lead local and national events, and connect employees with the resources they need to design, develop, and test their own solutions.

And now, iNET has created an Innovation Program Playbook to help external healthcare systems, federal agencies, and others develop their own employee-centric innovation programs.
